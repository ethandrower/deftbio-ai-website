Our mission

Compliant clinical ops, without the department

Deft gives lean biotech teams the training, signatures, and Trial Master File a serious trial needs — sized for the team you actually have.

Why we built Deft

The rules don't scale down

Clinical compliance tools were built for big pharma — heavy, costly, and run by teams a startup doesn't have. But a 12-person biotech answers to the same regulations as a 12,000-person one.

So lean teams improvise with spreadsheets and email until an audit forces a scramble. Deft closes that gap: the compliance backbone of a real trial, sized and priced for the team you have.
